About Dandelion Cavies


We are a passionate group of guinea pig lovers dedicated to sharing knowledge about these adorable creatures. Whether you’re a new guinea pig owner or an expert, we have something for everyone. Guinea pigs, also known as cavies, are small, social rodents native to the Andes in South America. They have been domesticated for over 3,000 years and were originally used as a food source, though today they are more commonly kept as pets due to their friendly nature and easy care.
